## Resource Allocation Strategy

### Where to Invest (Priority Order):


```

40% → Data Infrastructure & Quality
30% → RAG Pipeline & Retrieval
20% → Fine-Tuning
10% → Evaluation & Monitoring

```

### Why This Split?

* **Data quality** is the highest-leverage investment — bad data kills every downstream effort.
* **RAG** gives you speed-to-value with no retraining cost.
* **Fine-tuning** is your differentiation layer, not your starting point.
* **Evaluation** is non-negotiable — you cannot fundraise or sell without proof of improvement.

---

## The 3-Phase Business Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Quick Wins (Month 1)
**Investment:** Low | **ROI:** Immediate

* Deploy few-shot prompting using your best internal knowledge.
* Launch an internal copilot for one team (sales, support, or ops).
* Measure time saved per employee per week.
* **Business outcome:** Prove internal ROI before external commitment.

> 📊 **Target metric:** 2–3 hours saved per employee per week

---

### Phase 2 — Build the Moat (Month 2–3)
**Investment:** Medium | **ROI:** 3–6 months

* Build RAG pipeline on top of your proprietary dataset.
* Your data becomes a **living knowledge base** — updated continuously.
* The model now knows your products, clients, processes, and language.
* **Business outcome:** Product is now differentiated from any off-the-shelf AI tool.

> 📊 **Target metric:** 40% reduction in incorrect or hallucinated outputs

---

### Phase 3 — Compound the Advantage (Month 4–6)
**Investment:** Targeted | **ROI:** Long-term

* Fine-tune on curated, high-quality company interactions.
* Model begins to reflect your company's **judgment**, not just its knowledge.
* Deploy externally — customer-facing chatbot, partner portal, API product.
* **Business outcome:** Data flywheel activates — usage generates training data.

> 📊 **Target metric:** 50%+ accuracy improvement vs. baseline, monetizable product

## The Data Flywheel — Your Real Competitive Advantage


```

Users interact with LLM
↓
Model generates responses
↓
Users correct or validate outputs
↓
Corrections become new training data
↓
Model gets smarter
↓
Better model attracts more users
↓
↑ (repeat)

```

**This is the moat.** After 12 months of this loop, your model is trained on thousands of real, validated, domain-specific interactions. No competitor can replicate that without your users and your time.

## Resource Team Structure

For a lean startup, you need only **3 core roles:**


```

1. AI/ML Engineer        → Builds RAG + fine-tuning pipeline
2. Data Curator          → Cleans, labels, and maintains dataset quality
3. Product Owner (You)   → Defines use cases, measures business outcomes

```

* **Outsource:** Infrastructure (use managed vector DBs, cloud fine-tuning APIs).
* **Keep in-house:** Data curation and evaluation — that's where your IP lives.
