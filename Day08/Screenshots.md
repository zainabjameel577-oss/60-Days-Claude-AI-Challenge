## Conclusion

The Personal Environmental Dashboard project demonstrated how data, visualization, and AI-assisted development can be combined to create meaningful insights from environmental information. Through this project, I strengthened my skills in project planning, data analysis, dashboard design, and problem-solving while gaining practical experience in using Claude AI as a development assistant. The final dashboard successfully transformed raw environmental data into an accessible and informative tool for monitoring and understanding environmental conditions.
